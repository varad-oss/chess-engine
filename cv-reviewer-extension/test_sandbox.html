CV Reviewer Extension Sandbox

🚀 How to Load and Test the Extension:

  1. Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/
  2. In the top-right corner, toggle the Developer mode switch to ON.
  3. In the top-left corner, click the Load unpacked button.
  4. Select the folder: cv-reviewer-extension located in your workspace.
  5. Pin the extension in your toolbar and click its icon to configure your Gemini or OpenAI API Key.
  6. Return to this page, type a resume draft bullet point in any of the fields below, and hover/click the floating Analyze button that appears at the bottom-right of the textarea.
